Zoe

DC Taji's Alster Ego from Hodari SC FCh RATI

Basenjis are like potato chips, right? You can't have just one.

Zoe came home with me in February 2012. She was a show potential little girl born in Amarillo, TX with Sherry Heath and co-bred with Katie Campbell. When Zoe arrived at our home, Echo must've thought it was just a play date. They played and ran for literally 4 hours before they both passed out asleep.

Zoe is a true Dual Champion excelling in (and greatly enjoying) the show ring as well as the field. She is a charming girl with showmanship to spare. Zoe loves lure coursing and anything where she gets to chase the bunny. She's also shown potential (and a lot of heart) in oval racing.

She is just so full of JOY that its easy to fall in love with her... and many have.
